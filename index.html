About Us

At DevJan Palmistry, we specialize in child psychology, parenting coaching, relationship counseling, and mental health support. Our mission is to help individuals and families navigate challenges such as anger, anxiety, depression, and marital issues, fostering emotional well-being and personal growth. Founded by Archana Bhatt, a gold medalist in palmistry from the All India Institute of Occult Science, Delhi, we bring a unique blend of psychological insight and spiritual guidance. With expertise in Vastu Shastra and KP Astrology, we have successfully guided over 200 clients across India and abroad, providing them with clarity and direction in their lives. We believe in empowering individuals by offering personalized solutions to help them overcome obstacles and achieve mental, emotional, and spiritual balance. Whether you're seeking guidance in relationships, personal development, or holistic well-being, we are here to support you on your journey.
